What?

In a Mixed Gaming Tournamen (MGT) players compete across several different rounds of competition, with each round played on a different game. These games include console video games, arcade games, board games, card games, and word games.

Why?

  • Gamers play games
  • There aren't a lot of compeitions where non-pro players can enjoy themselves
  • We can raise some money for really good causes
