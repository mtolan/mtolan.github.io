In addition to the rules specific to each individual game, the Tournament at large will have additional rules. The sole intention of these rules is to provide a fun and welcoming environment that encourages players to come round again the next time we have a tournament.

Rules

1. Don't be a dick

Enforcement

Violations of the tournament code of conduct will be sanctioned similar to the given in soccer matches. Possible penalties include

1. Warning

Players can be warned that a particular action is in violation of the code of conduct, and that a repeat occurrence will lead to more significant sanctions.

2. Yellow Card

A player that continues a particular violation after a warning, or who willfully violates the code of conduct where a warning would be inadequate is shown a yellow card. A second yellow card received during a single round results in an automatic red card.

3. Red Card

A player who is shown a red card is immediately disqualified from the round currently in progress, and required to vacate the immediate area where the round is being conducted. The player will receive a score of 0.0 for that round.

4. Black Card

Players who are shown a black card by tournament staff are immediately disqualified from the tournament and will be immediately escorted from the premises by a member of Tournament Security. A player shown a black card will be ineligible to compete in events for not less than 12 months.
